About Us

Leela Games Entertainment Studios is a one-man indie game studio based in Hyderabad, Telangana. Founded by Senior 3D Artist Mr. Tejas Shankpal, our mission is to create family-friendly cross-platform games that bring joy to children and parents alike. We support new talent and aim to build creative, immersive digital experiences.
